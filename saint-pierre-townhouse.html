Saint-Pierre 🇫🇷

Jul 20 → 244 nights · actual France

Getting there: Air Saint-Pierre PJ1122 direct from Halifax, Mon Jul 20, 3:30p → 5:45p (quoted refundable, C$3,102.64 for all four) · ~1¼ hrs · passports required — this is actual France: euros, EU plugs, boulangeries. Home base: the 3BR townhouse, walkable to everything.

🏠 The find — booked & confirmed (Jul 2)

An entire 3-bedroom townhouse — sleeps 5, 3 beds, 1.5 baths, 4.93★ over 30 reviews, Superhost, $840 for 3 nights all-in, free cancellation before Jul 19 — available Jul 20–23. ✅ Booked. That's the whole reason this window works when the island's lodging is otherwise nearly gone.

⚠️ The one catch — Thursday Jul 23 night

Checkout is the 23rd, the flight home is Friday the 24th at 9:00a, and no flight leaves Saint-Pierre toward Halifax on a Thursday — so one night to bridge. Best to worst: 1) ask the host to extend to the 24th · 2) one hotel night for the 23rd (a single night ×4 is an easy ask) · 3) re-date the refundable return to the Wed Jul 22 9:00a flight (France drops to 2 full days, PEI gains two) · 4) fly out Thursday via Montréal — noon SPM→YUL, a few hours downtown, late YUL→Halifax — which skips the bridge night entirely for a pricier ticket (see the alternative timeline above). If you'd rather turn that Thursday into a Newfoundland leg, that's Plan E.

🍽️ Good to know: summer dinner reservations are near-mandatory — restaurants book up the same week as the rooms. Île aux Marins and a Miquelon day are the set pieces.
