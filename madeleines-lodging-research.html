Appartement de la Grave (Havre-Aubert) — 1 bedroom, sleeps 2 max — doesn't meet the 2-bedroom requirement.
Resorts-accommodation & Bed & Breakfasts categories — checked both; resorts returned only group/camp-style venues (nautical center, park lodge, auberge — dorm-adjacent, skipped per "no hostels"), and B&Bs returned zero results for 4 people in a single booking (B&B rooms there are sized for 1–2, and the site doesn't bundle multiple rooms into one search match).

Bottom line

Appartements Cyrco #1 is the strongest option — it's the only property that cleared every span tested, has the loosest minimum-stay policy (2 nights), and has real guest reviews. Chez Aurèle/Raynald à Arsène is a strong second (same price range, 2 separate 2BR houses to choose from) if the Jul 20–24 window holds. Chez Arthur à Azade is the fallback if you want a 3rd bedroom. La Maison Verte reads perfectly on paper but needs a direct call given its stated 1–2 week minimum — worth asking since off-peak-adjacent 4-night exceptions do happen with small owner-operators.

Next step, if you want to move on this: call/email Cyrco and Aurèle in parallel to lock in whichever confirms first — both are ~$1,300–1,320 for the 4 nights, in line with the plan's existing $700 lodging estimate... actually above it, so if one of these books, the Plan D islands-only cost should be updated (current page assumes $700/4 nights ÷ shared across the group — these run $1,300+ before splitting).
