03

St. John's, Newfoundland 🇨🇦

Jul 23 → 263 nights · by ferry + shuttle

Getting there: the crossing that makes this plan — SPM Ferries, Saint-Pierre → Fortune, NL, Thu Jul 23 at 10:00a (~1h30, verified on the 2026 calendar). Then pick up the Avalon by road.

⚠️ Fortune → St. John's — the standard schedule doesn't match our ferry

Fortune sits at the tip of the Burin Peninsula — 360 km / ~4¾ hrs from St. John's, with no car-rental counter at the wharf. The shuttle we're counting on is Foote's / Matthews Taxi & Bus — but their posted daily run departs the peninsula early (~6:00a Fortune, ~6:20a Grand Bank, ~7:00a Marystown), timed for morning flights out of St. John's. Our ferry doesn't land in Fortune until ~11:30a that Thursday — the regular van will already be gone.

What this means: either (1) they can run a special afternoon pickup timed to the ferry — call and ask, this is the first move — or (2) we overnight near Fortune/Grand Bank/Marystown and catch the 6a van Friday instead, which pushes the whole rest of the trip back a day, or (3) we find a one-way rental car out of Marystown (the largest town on the peninsula, ~45 min from Fortune) — not yet checked.

Contact: office/cancellations 709-832-0491 (also 1-800-866-1181) · driver's cell 709-682-5142 · Facebook page. Backups if theirs can't work: R&E Transportation (709-891-1866) or Da Boot Bussing (709-567-4703).

Their booking policy (per their own July post): pickup times are approximate and depend on how many stops that run has — be ready, the driver may or may not call ahead. St. John's-bound pickups there run 11a–12p on weekends, 1:00–1:30p on weekdays (their return leg down the peninsula). If you book by phone/email in advance, they're now requiring payment ~1 week before travel to hold the seat, and no refund without 24 hours' cancellation notice — so don't lock this in until the timing is actually confirmed.

🚗 Pick up a rental car at St. John's airport (YYT) for the few days on the Avalon — cheap and plentiful.

Worth it: Signal Hill, Cape Spear (easternmost point in North America), Quidi Vidi, whales offshore in July.

🧭 Heads up: NL time is 30 minutes ahead of Atlantic, and Saint-Pierre is 30 minutes behind — the short ferry hop crosses a full hour. 🛏️ St. John's has real hotel + Airbnb inventory (easy, unlike Saint-Pierre).
