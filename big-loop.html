02

Saint-Pierre 🇫🇷

Jul 20 → 233 nights · the townhouse, one-way

Getting there: Air Saint-Pierre PJ1122 direct from Halifax, Mon Jul 20, 3:30p → 5:45p · ~1¼ hrs · passports required — this is actual France. Home base: the booked 3BR townhouse. In this plan it's one-way — we don't fly back, we sail onward.

✅ Why this plan dissolves the bridge night

The townhouse runs Jul 20–23 and checkout is Thursday the 23rd — the exact morning the SPM Ferries boat sails to Fortune, NL at 10:00a. So we check out and step straight onto it. No hotel night to solve; the ferry is the exit.

🍽️ Good to know: three nights is tighter than the townhouse plan — Île aux Marins and a Miquelon day, then out. Summer dinner reservations are near-mandatory.
03

St. John's, Newfoundland 🇨🇦

Jul 23 → 263 nights · by ferry + shuttle

Getting there: the crossing that makes this plan — SPM Ferries, Saint-Pierre → Fortune, NL, Thu Jul 23 at 10:00a (~1h30, verified on the 2026 calendar). Then pick up the Avalon by road.

🚐 Fortune → St. John's — the one stretch to arrange

Fortune sits at the tip of the Burin Peninsula — 360 km / ~4¾ hrs from St. John's, with no car-rental counter at the wharf. The clean answer is a daily shuttle van: Foote's Taxi & Bus (~$70/pp · 1-800-866-1181 / 709-832-0491), or R&E Transportation (709-891-1866) / Da Boot Bussing (709-567-4703). Book ahead — small vans. 🚗 Pick up a rental car at St. John's airport (YYT) for the few days on the Avalon — cheap and plentiful.

Worth it: Signal Hill, Cape Spear (easternmost point in North America), Quidi Vidi, whales offshore in July.

🧭 Heads up: NL time is 30 minutes ahead of Atlantic, and Saint-Pierre is 30 minutes behind — the short ferry hop crosses a full hour. 🛏️ St. John's has real hotel + Airbnb inventory (easy, unlike Saint-Pierre).
