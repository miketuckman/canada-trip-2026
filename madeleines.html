The shape

Out to the Gulf and back — no border crossing

Two nights in Halifax (arrival + a Peggy's Cove day), the Wood Islands ferry to PEI on the 17th for a few days with our friends, then the CTMA ferry from Souris — 40 minutes from their place — five hours across the Gulf to Québec's Îles de la Madeleine for four nights. Back to PEI on the morning boat, a last couple of days, and home out of Halifax on the 26th. Simplest travel of any plan — one country, one time zone, every leg a daily ferry — but it hinges on two open questions: walk-on seats and a car on the island.

01

Halifax, Nova Scotia

Jul 15 → 172 nights up front

Getting there: Orlando → Halifax, Wed Jul 15, United/Air Canada via Chicago · 7:09a → 3:58p · Economy + 1 checked bag. Pick up one rental car for the whole trip at the airport.

Land, settle in, and give the 16th to Halifax — the harbour plus a Peggy's Cove run (~1 hr out). Then on Fri Jul 17 drive ~1¾ hrs to Caribou and take the Wood Islands ferry to PEI.

🛏️ Stay: 2 nights in/near Halifax — deep hotel inventory, book last.
02

Prince Edward Island

Jul 17 → 20 & Jul 24 → 26in two acts · our friends' home turf

Getting there: the Wood Islands ferry on Fri Jul 17 (75 min, 8 daily sailings, toll-free toward PEI) lands us at our friends' end of the island. Act one is the warm-up; act two is everything we didn't get to, after the Madeleines.

⛴️ Wood Islands ferry — Caribou, NS → Wood Islands, PEI

8 sailings a day, 75-min crossing. Departures from Caribou: 7:00 · 8:30 · 10:00 · 11:45 · 1:30 · 3:15 · 5:00 · 6:30. No vehicle reservations — first-come, arrive ~45–60 min early on a summer Friday — and it's toll-free heading to PEI (you only pay leaving the island).

🛏️ Stay: with friends / their picks — they're the hosts, so lodging here is effectively free. 🚗 The rental parks at the Souris terminal for the four days we're on the Madeleines.
03

Îles de la Madeleine ⚜️

Jul 20 → 244 nights · Québec, 5 hrs into the Gulf

Getting there: CTMA ferry from Souris — 40 minutes east of our friends' place — sails ~1:00p Mon Jul 20, docks at Cap-aux-Meules ~6:15p; the return sails ~7:00a Fri Jul 24 and has us back on PEI for lunch. Both boats run daily, so these dates can slide.

🚗 Update (Jul 3): Rental car may be an option after all — checking with the island operator now. Update later today.

⛔ The open question (Jul 2): car decks are SOLD OUT — walk-on or bust

CTMA's booking engine shows no vehicle space on the ferry for our entire window, either direction. Bringing the rental is (currently) dead. The plan survives as walk-on passengers, which needs two confirmations:

1️⃣ Passenger seats on our two sailings — out Souris→Cap-aux-Meules Mon Jul 20 (~1:00p), back CaM→Souris Fri Jul 24 (7:00a) — foot-passenger space usually outlasts the car deck. Verify and book: 1-888-986-3278 / traversierctma.ca.

2️⃣ Wheels on the island — Agence de Location des Îles (cars, scooters; town + airport offices · 1-888-818-4537). Our window sits inside Québec's construction holiday (Jul 19–Aug 1), the islands' peak — scooters (~$90/day) are the fallback.

If either check fails, one of the Saint-Pierre plans becomes the plan.

🧭 Good to know: the Madeleines are the one corner of Québec on Atlantic time — no clock change from PEI. The crossing is ~5 hours each way; bring a book and watch for whales. 🛏️ Island lodging for 4 nights (tourismilesdelamadeleine.com · Château Madelinot · Auberge Madeli).
