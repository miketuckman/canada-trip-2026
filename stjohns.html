01

Halifax, Nova Scotia

Jul 15 → 17 & Jul 202 nights up front + the pre-flight night

Getting there: Orlando → Halifax, Wed Jul 15, United/Air Canada via Chicago · 7:09a → 3:58p · Economy + 1 checked bag ≈ $432 total for two. Pick up a rental car at the airport.

Two nights up front (Jul 15–16): land, settle in, and give the 16th to Halifax — the harbour plus a Peggy's Cove run (~1 hr out). Then on Fri Jul 17 drive ~1¾ hrs to Caribou and take the Wood Islands ferry to PEI (schedule below). Halifax comes back once more: overnight near YHZ on Jul 20 so we can catch the early St. John's flight.

⛴️ Wood Islands ferry — Fri Jul 17 · Caribou, NS → Wood Islands, PEI

8 sailings that day, 75-min crossing. Departures from Caribou: 7:00 · 8:30 · 10:00 · 11:45 · 1:30 · 3:15 · 5:00 · 6:30. A mid-afternoon boat (1:30 or 3:15) fits a relaxed Halifax morning plus the drive and lands us at our friends' by dinner. No vehicle reservations — first-come, so arrive ~45–60 min early on a summer Friday — and it's toll-free heading to PEI (you only pay leaving the island).

🛏️ Stay: 2 nights in/near Halifax (Jul 15–16) + 1 airport night Jul 20 — deep hotel inventory, book last. 🚗 The rental goes back at YHZ before we fly to St. John's — no car idling while we're on the Rock.
02

Prince Edward Island

Jul 17 → 203 nights · our friends' home turf

Getting there: the Wood Islands ferry on Fri Jul 17 (day's sailings listed on the Halifax card above) lands us at our friends' end of the island. On Jul 20 we reverse it — a morning ferry back to Caribou, drive to Halifax, and overnight by the airport for the early St. John's flight.

🛏️ Stay: with friends / their picks — they're the hosts, so lodging here is effectively free. The easy middle of the trip: beaches, lobster, back roads on their schedule.
03

St. John's, Newfoundland 🇨🇦

Jul 21 → 243 nights · nonstop from Halifax

Getting there: Air Canada nonstop both ways — out Tue Jul 21, 8:05a → 10:28a (1h53), back Fri Jul 24, 7:30a → 9:09a (2h09). Pick up a rental car at St. John's airport (YYT) — cheap and plentiful. Hotels/Airbnbs here are easy, unlike the other plans' islands.

Worth the short days (no detours needed): Signal Hill, Cape Spear (the easternmost point in North America), Quidi Vidi, and whales offshore in July. Gander or Bonavista only if you stretch the stay.

⏱️ The tight bit — Jul 24 same-day connection

We land back in Halifax at 9:09a on Jul 24 and fly Halifax → Orlando the same afternoon (2:50p) — a comfortable ~5-hour buffer. The earlier/cheaper YHZ→MCO options leave at 6–7a and don't work with the morning arrival from St. John's, so the 2:50p departure is the realistic one.

🛏️ Stay: 3 nights in St. John's (hotel or Airbnb — real inventory, book when the flights lock). 🚗 Separate rental car at YYT for the Avalon.
